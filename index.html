History

The Durham House of Pets was established in 1956 and has been a local favorite for its family friendly atmosphere. It was founded by Dan and Susan Gallery when they decided to use their love for animals to create their own Pet Store. When DHP first opened, they sold every live animal from dogs and cats to chickens. Along with the live animals, the store used to sell mostly farming supplies. A large portion of their store was dedicated to everything one would need for running their own farm with grain to feed the animals and the necessary equipment for running a successful farm. Times have changed since the store opened a mere 63 years ago and has shifted its focus to selling more common domestic animal products. We have since stopped selling live animals in order to ensure these young animals are not kept living in a store until they are adopted. With the farming industry becoming less popular, we shifted our focus to selling supplies for cats, dogs and fish.

Why use us?

If you are tired of the high prices and rude staff at commercial pet stores, DHP is the one for you! Our staff consists of the next few generations of the Gallery family along with long time friends of the family. The store has a very fun and family friendly atmosphere with prices that wont break your wallet. If you're lost in what you need for your pet, one of our staff members would be more than happy to take you around the store to figure out just what you need. We have always been known for our great customer service and cheap prices!
