Delivery

DHP is well known for it's fast delivery policy of a guaranteed delivery within 48 hours of your purchase. If for any reason you do not recieve your order within that time frame, we will give you a 50% discount of the late order. We also have free frate for all orders that are a minimum of $25. Our store also offers a membership system for dog and cat owners that ships a box filled with a variety of treats and toys every month right to your door step.

Return

If you wish to return an item to our facility, we will provide a return label for you and will cover the shipping cost. If you return your item within seven business days from the day it was delivered, we will fully refund your order. If you return your order any later, we will only be able to provide a refund in the form of frequent flyer points. Every 5 points counts as $1 towards a future oorder.
